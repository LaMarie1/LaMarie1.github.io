Bees in the Media

There are so many famous bees in the media, we just had to take the time to recognize their hard work and achievement.

Barry B. Benson

Academy award nominated actor, comedian, and star of perhaps one of the most poigant and influence films of our generation, The Bee Movie (2007), Barry is a honeybee who falls in love with a human woman named Vanessa and generated a lot of buzz as he taught the world to accept beestiality.

The Honey Nut Cheerios Bee

Beloved mascot of the most well known Cheerios brand, this bee, known in some hives as BuzzBee, has been promoting healthy eating and a love for bees in the minds of children since 1979.

BumbleBee Man

This random side character from The Simpsons never takes off his bee costume is the star of a Spanish-language TV show where he performs slapstick comedy. His parents died playing paintball. He is a true hero and we salute him.
